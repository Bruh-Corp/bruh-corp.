EYE OF
SAURON

Eye Of Sauron is a multidisciplinary project involving elements of robotics, remote sensing, sensor integration, and rapid prototyping.

The project is an Unmanned Ground Vehicle (UGV) making use of Multimodal Sensor Fusion (Infrared, TV, Ultrasonic, Radar) for reconnaissance purposes.
